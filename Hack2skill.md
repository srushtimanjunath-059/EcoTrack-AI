# 🌿 EcoTrack AI

EcoTrack AI is a smart carbon footprint tracking system designed to help individuals understand, monitor, and reduce their environmental impact through simple inputs and personalized insights.

## 🚀 Features
- Carbon footprint calculation
- Daily activity tracking (travel, electricity, food)
- Personalized eco recommendations
- Progress dashboard concept

## 🧠 Idea Goal
To simplify sustainability by converting daily actions into a measurable carbon score and actionable improvements.

## 🎨 Prototype
This repository contains:
- UI/UX prototype (Canva design)
- Presentation deck
- System design flow

## 🌍 Vision
To make sustainability simple, visual, and part of everyday decision-making.
